About

I'm a second-year M.S. student of Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University. Now I work in Websoft Research Group, supervised by Associate Prof. Gong Cheng and Prof. Yuzhong Qu.

My C.V. is available here.

Research Interest

My research interset lies in Semantic Web and Knowledge Graph for intelligent software systems.

Nowadays with the rapid growth of open data on the Web, dataset search (or dataset retrieval) is gradually becoming a significant problem. I mainly study the semantic search algorithms and techniques used in dataset search systems, including data summarization, query analysis, and information retrieval.
